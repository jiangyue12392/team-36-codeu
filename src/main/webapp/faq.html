FAQ Page

Just like the name suggests, this website is exclusively created for moviegoers to share their passion for the cine world with fellow admirers!

This is a free and non-judgemental space, meant solely to connect with like-minded people and share your love for the movies. Hope you enjoy using this cool website.

We're glad you asked! You can view the many different theatres in Singapore as markers on a map. Select the ones you have visited or want to know more about, and leave your review and comments for the same! Alternatively, simply read through what others are saying about specific theatres and movies and chime in your thoughts.

You can access the maps page by clicking on 'Maps' in the navigation bar. We currently have three cool modes - regular, night and sattelite. Select whichever one you want!

Each of the theatres in Singapore is displayed as a marker on this map. You can select the ones you are interested in. Click on the marker will open a pop-up tab with space to enter your comments. One you are satisfied with your message, simply hit submit! It's that simple.

When you submit a review, you will automatically be taken to the feeds page. Here, you can read through all the review for the same threatre made about fellow users of the website.

You can also translate the messages in the feed using the button on top!!

One of the novel features of our website is the sentiment score analysis. Each review posted is analysed by our smart servers to generate a 'sentiment score'. This score ranges from -1 to 1, based on how negative/positive the language you used in your comment is.

Based on these individual scores, we have generated an aggregate value for each theatre, which is the number you see. This will allow you to quickly guage how other users are feeling about the chosen theatre! Pretty cool, isn't it?
